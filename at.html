Motorcycle
A motorcycle is a two-wheeled vehicle with a more upright seating position and typically a manual transmission. It is popular for its performance and versatility.
Hatchback
A hatchback is a type of car with a rear door that opens upward, providing access to a cargo area. It is compact and ideal for city driving.
Sedan
A sedan is a passenger car with separate compartments for engine, passengers, and cargo. It offers comfort and style for long-distance travel.
MUV / SUV
MUV (Multi Utility Vehicle) and SUV (Sports Utility Vehicle) are larger vehicles designed for family passengers and cargo, often featuring robust builds and off-road capability.
Electric Vehicle (EV)
Electric vehicles are powered entirely by batteries instead of internal combustion engines, making them eco-friendly and cost-efficient for daily use.
